Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job on JobFinder?

To post a job, you need to create an employer account, go to your dashboard, and click on "Post a New Job". Follow the instructions to complete your job listing.

How can I update my resume?

Log in to your account, navigate to "My Profile", then select "Manage Resume" where you can upload a new version or edit your existing resume.

What are the payment options for employers?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise clients, we also offer invoice-based payments.

How long will my job posting remain active?

Standard job postings remain active for 30 days. Premium listings can be extended up to 60 days.
